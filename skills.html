My UX Skills

User Research

User Research is the foundation to creating a solution/product that truly solves a problem. I pride myself in always creating a concrete research plan to lead that leads to valuable research findings.

User Empathy

Users are at the center of all that we do. My approach is to always understand the "why" behind their thinking and create a safe space for them to express their creativity in addition to their concerns.

User Testing

User Testing is what enables us to iterate on ideas and create more user centered designs. Creating a comfortable environment for users is essential in receiving valuable feedback.
